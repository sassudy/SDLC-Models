Scrum: Advantages and Disadvantages

Advantages Disadvantages
Scrum can help teams complete project deliverables quickly and efficiently Adopting the Scrum framework in large teams is challenging
Scrum ensures effective use of time and money The framework can be successful only with experienced team members
Large projects are divided into easily manageable sprints If any team member leaves in the middle of a project, it can have a huge negative impact on the project
